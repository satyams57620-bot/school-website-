About School

Established in 1952, Gopal Vidyalaya Inter College [English Medium] is one of the most respected educational institutions of Koraon, Prayagraj. The school follows the UP Board curriculum and provides education from Nursery to Class XII. Students of Classes XI and XII can choose PCM, PCB, Commerce and Arts streams according to their career goals. Our institution focuses on academic excellence, discipline, character building and overall development of students. With modern facilities and dedicated teachers, the school continues its mission of producing knowledgeable and responsible citizens.
