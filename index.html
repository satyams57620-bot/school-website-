FROM THE PRINCIPAL'S DESK

Dear Readers,

School life is very important part of our life because it provides opportunity for study, discipline and progress. The school life is a preparatory stage for entering public life. Education is not gaining about a degree but it is about how can we live on our feet. We live today in a world that is very different from the past. The world today is changing at such an acceleration of the future.

As the Principal of the school I feel honoured and privileged to be a part of such educational institution where every person is a learner and everyday is an opportunity to learn and discover. We look ourselves as a community of learners where everyone learns including students, teachers, parents and staff.

Gopal Vidyalaya Inter College Koraon (Hindi and English medium) has also established its reputation in imparting quality education. The school always tries its best to maintain the highest quality academic standards and provide the wonderful environment for students. The aim of our school is to provide unmatched qualitative education. Our approach to learning is child centric which encourages activity-based learning in academics, sports and co-curricular activities. We are doing our best to prepare the institution with a clear vision of educational intentions.

We are constantly improving our teaching method, developing infrastructural facilities and resources so that the students can achieve their goal. I invite more suggestions from the learned persons of the area, parents and well-wishers of the school. Our goal is to discover and nurture the unique abilities of the students and transforming him/her to be a confident and responsible citizen.

I am grateful to the Chairperson of management committee Rajmata Mrs. Seeta Kumari, Manager of this institution honourable Raja Bahadur Raja Ajeya Singh and honourable vice-president of Gopal Vidyalaya Management Committee Mr. R.N. Bajpai for their grace and guidance to the institution, editorial committee of this magazine, management committee, all the learned teachers, non-teaching staff, students and well-wishers of the college.

Thanking you,

Dr. Mohd. Sabir Ali
